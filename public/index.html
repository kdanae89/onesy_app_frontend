What the heck is Project Onesie though?!

I recently became a mother, and all I wanted was to dress my little peanut up in the cutest nerd attire. On Amazon you can find a ton of Star Wars garb and anything pikachu, but the options are surprisingly limited... I wished someone had made an affordable app where I could make my own! That's the point of Project Onesie, to give parents the option to nerdify their little ones and possibly humiliate them later on in life.

Instructions!

First things first, if you haven't, register and log-in so that your onesie is is custom to you. The space for that is up above in the yellow box. Then fill out the form and click "create onesie" and you're on your way! Just drag any desireable image to your onesie and plop it on where ever you like!
