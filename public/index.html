Make Your Onesie! Let's get started!

What the heck is Project Onesie though?!

Being a recent first time parent, I wanted to dress my son in all the cutest nerdy gear! On Amazon, you can find Star Wars and select few Pokemon onesies (obviously) but I didn't want such limited options. I decided to create an app in which parents (or enthusiasts?) could create a onesie with a design of their choosing for their little one's wardrobe. Now it's possible to not only deck your baby out in the coolest garb, but to subliminally create that awesome nerdy friend you always wanted. Totally kidding.

Instructions!

First thing's first, you will have to register an account. This is so that we can track your onesie when you ultimately send it off for design. After registering, go ahead and log in to start creating. You can select a onesie from our list of color options via Create A Onesie form, and get to decorating! The images to the right of your onesie are draggable, so just drag them over to the desired location on your onesy and there you have it! A customized onesie!
